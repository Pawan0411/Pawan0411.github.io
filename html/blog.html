5th June, 2020

My note is to all 3rd years guys 2021 batch. Placements season has been reopen. So get ready to face different kind of challenges. This is the time when you will get various ups and downs in your life. But it is not necessary all will get selected/hired some will get hired some will get another chance in there life. Those who have achieved success at their first try congratulations to them, but Those who haven't achieved success, you will get another opportunity in life. You need to wait for the right time, don't let negative things to come in your mind. After all it's your mind or it's you only who is responsible for all the things, for your success for your failure. Rather you should not use the word "Failure" just interpret it as a "way that didn't worked, I need to find the different way". Yes It's possible your previous way is also correct but somethings life ask for perfect things too. It give us chance to become perfect.

A common situation comes in engineering students, student S has started preparing for the exam or interview more earlier than his classmate. Now, Student A and his classmates gave the same exam or have appeared for same company as according to previous records yes Student A have more marks than my classmate, he have done more things ae compared to his classes etc. But ultimately, his classmates get the job first. Here's the situation where student A feels like frustrated, irritated and many more. But is this correct? No. But that's how life is - The more you want something, the more you chase for something god will keep taking it away and that's because he has planned something more better for you. Student A kept preparing more and more he never gave up . Tough time only makes him more strong. Once again he tried for some company X but again the same thing happened his roommates where placed but he wasn't.

He was happy for them, it wasn't jealousy, we think we are jealous but that feeling was different that's sadness when you try really hard and you don't get things. It hurts when you work so hard but god keeps giving to someone who already have something, it's normal to feel upset and sad but don't let yourself soak in it.

Because God makes difficult situation more difficult just to see your patience and how much you are determined towards working hard.

so just remember the more rejection you get chances of your success increases. don't let your failure define you. cut all the contacts with people who criticize you and makes you feel demotivated.

One lesson : "sometimes late success is a blessing in disguise".

And yes finally Student A get job after all his struggle and belive me it will always something more than the companies you got rejected.

All the failure and struggles are basically a blessing it does not only gives you success it makes you a strong person too.

That's all Guys,
Dont stop when you are tired stop when you are done.
All the best for your career!
